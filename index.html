HOTEL GALWAY

Welcome to Galway Hotel

Looking for a place that fits your budget and your vibe? Galway Hotel is designed for students and young travelers who want more for less. Affordable, stylish rooms with all the essentials—cozy beds, kitchenettes, laundry facilities, and lightning-fast Wi-Fi—make it easy to study, chill, or explore Galway. Plus, we’re in the heart of the action, close to colleges, nightlife, and everything Galway has to offer. Whether you’re here for a semester or just a few nights, Galway Hotel is where great stays and even better memories start.
