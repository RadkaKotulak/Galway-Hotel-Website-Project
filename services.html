HOTEL SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

GYM

Stay fit and active in our fully equipped gym, open 24/7 for residents. Whether you prefer cardio, weightlifting, or yoga, our gym has something for everyone. Access to the gym is included in your rent, making it a convenient and cost-effective way to maintain your health and fitness.

LAUNDRY

Our 24/7 laundry facilities feature modern washing machines and dryers to keep your clothes fresh and clean. Conveniently located within the building, the laundry area operates on a pay-per-use system, ensuring flexibility and easy access whenever you need it.

CO-WORKING

Our spacious and comfortable co-working area is designed to inspire productivity. Open 24/7, it’s equipped with ergonomic seating, ample power outlets, and high-speed internet. Perfect for group projects, remote work, or quiet study sessions.

CANTEEN

Fuel your day with delicious meals from our canteen, open daily from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Enjoy a variety of healthy and affordable options, including vegetarian and vegan dishes. The canteen is a perfect place to grab a quick bite or enjoy a relaxed meal with friends.

TERRACE

Relax and unwind on our beautifully designed terraces, open daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Equipped with seating and greenery, these spaces are perfect for studying, socializing, or simply enjoying a moment of peace.

CAR PARK

We offer secure parking facilities exclusively for residents. Parking spaces can be rented on a monthly basis for €50, providing peace of mind and easy access to your vehicle whenever you need it. Spaces are limited, so early booking is recommended.

RECEPTION

Our friendly reception team is available from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily to assist with any queries, maintenance requests, or bookings. Whether you need directions, help with your room, or just a friendly face to talk to, we’re here to help.

VENDING MACHINE

Located on every floor, our vending machines are stocked with a variety of snacks and drinks. Available 24/7, they offer a quick and convenient way to grab a bite between study sessions or late at night.

POST

Each resident is assigned a secure post box for mail and small packages. The post box area is accessible 24/7, and larger parcels can be collected at reception during its operating hours.
