Frequently asked questions

1. Who can book a room at Galway Hotel?

2. How do I book a room?
You can easily book a room online through our website. Just select your dates, room type, and complete the process in a few clicks.
3. What facilities are included?
Wi-Fi, parking, laundry facilities, a gym, a canteen, and a coworking area.
4. Is there a minimum stay duration?
For students, a minimum stay of one semester is required. Short-term travelers can book for as little as one night.
6. Are utilities included in the rent?
All utilities, including electricity, water, and Wi-Fi, are included in the price.
8. Is the property safe?
Absolutely. We have 24/7 security, CCTV monitoring, and key-card access.
9. Can I bring guests?
Visitors are welcome but must follow our guest policy. Overnight stays may require prior approval.
10. Is the canteen food included in the rent?
No, the canteen operates separately, offering affordable meals at student-friendly prices.
11. Is parking free?
Yes, on-site parking is free for our guests.
12. Can I bring my pet?
Unfortunately, we do not allow pets, except for certified service animals.
If you have additional questions, feel free to contact us anytime!
