STUDENT ACCOMODATION
At Galway Hotel, we prioritize providing a secure and student-friendly environment. To book a room, ensure you meet the following criteria:

Eligibility Requirements:
  1. Student Identification:
      2. Valid student ID or proof of enrollment from an accredited institution (e.g., University of Galway, GMIT, or other local universities).
  2. Age Range:
      3. Accommodation is available exclusively for individuals aged 18–30.
  3. Additional Documentation:
      4. A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver’s license, or national ID).
  4. Security and Booking Fees:
    • Security Deposit: €250 (fully refundable upon checkout, subject to room inspection).
    • Booking Fee: €50 (non-refundable to secure your reservation).
  5. Booking Process :
    • Submit required documents through our website or via email.
    • Confirm your booking and enjoy a smooth move-in process!
